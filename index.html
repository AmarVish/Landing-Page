About the Black Pearl

“Black Pearl” is the name of a fictional pirate ship in the Pirates of the Caribbean movie franchise. It is captained by Jack Sparrow, a cunning and charismatic pirate who often gets into trouble with his enemies and allies. The ship is famous for its speed, maneuverability, and cursed crew who turn into skeletons in the moonlight.
